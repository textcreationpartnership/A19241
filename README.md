    _ A pleasant tale of an Vsurer.

    _ A pleasant Tale of a Miller and an Alewiues Boy

    _ A pleasant Tale of Will Sommers.

for a Wife.

    _ A pleasaunt Tale of a man that was marryed to sixteene Wiues,
